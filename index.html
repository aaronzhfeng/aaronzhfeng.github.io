About

I'm currently a senior undergraduate at UC San Diego pursuing a double major in Data Science and Probability & Statistics (GPA 3.83).

My research interests lie at the intersection of Machine Learning Systems and Scientific AI. I love diving deep into how we can make LLMs more efficient through memory systems and optimizing training pipelines for open-source models.

Outside of the lab, I'm usually analyzing datasets (sometimes just for fun), or exploring creative coding through algorithmic music generation.
